Sport-N-Support     offers an efficient way to give and get academic help privately.  All student-athletes can reach greater results in their schoolwork and sports through this site. Just visit it to easily offer or request help in specific subjects of choice. Then maximize your time during the busy sports season!

            Reasons t o  ask for help :

  • Improve grades or keep them high
  • Relieve any anxiety before tests
  • Retain academic eligibility
  • Perform better on the field and in class
  • Avoid having to miss games or practices
  • Work at your own pace
  • Build new levels of trust and friendship

            Reasons to offer help :

  • Give back to your team mates and community
  • Help out someone who needs it
  • Gain leadership and volunteer experience
  • Improve your time management skills
  • Build and enhance your resume
  • Practice your subject matter expertise
  • Earn volunteer hours  ?

 

          Join in 5 easy steps:

  1. Go to http://snsweb.azurewebsites.net  
  2. Register with your email and select your team(s).  
  3. Choose the subjects in which to give or get help.
  4. View your matches.

               Each time you login the matches will be updated.

  1. Contact each other and start helping/getting help!
